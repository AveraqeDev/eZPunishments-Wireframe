Welcome to eZPunishments Panel!

Here you are able to easily manage the players, and their punishments, on your Minecraft Server!
To get started you must login with the default user(`admin`) and password(`admin`). Once logged in,
you can navigate to the `Punishments` page to view a list of all punishments executed, to
the `Users` page to view a list of registered users, or to the `Moderation` tab to execute a punishment.
